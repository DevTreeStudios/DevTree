Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 10, 2025

Who We Are

DevTree Studios is a U.S.-based development service operated solely by an individual developer. We build websites, software, and provide tutoring services. We do not sell or share user data beyond the use of standard third-party tools explained below.

Information We Collect

We collect limited information through third-party services for functionality and analytics:

  • Google Analytics: Tracks general usage patterns such as page visits, browser type, and time spent on the site. This may include approximate location data based on your IP address. While we do not personally identify users, this information may be considered personal under some privacy laws.
  • Google reCAPTCHA v3: Protects contact forms from spam and abuse. It may collect hardware and software information, such as device and browser details, for security purposes.
  • Google Drive Video Embeds: Videos embedded from Google Drive may set cookies necessary for playback. These cookies are controlled by Google.
  • Formspree (Contact Form): When you submit a message through our contact page, Formspree securely handles the form data and forwards it to our email inbox. This may include your name, email, and message content. We do not store this information on our website. For more details, refer to Formspree’s Privacy Policy.

Cookies

This site uses cookies to enhance your experience and provide video playback and analytics. Cookies are only set after you give explicit consent. The cookies used include:

  • Cookies from Google Analytics to track website performance
  • Cookies from Google Drive to allow video playback
  • Cookies from Google reCAPTCHA to protect forms

Consent

You are asked to consent to the use of cookies when you first visit this site. By clicking "Accept," you agree to the use of cookies as outlined in this policy. You may withdraw your consent at any time by clearing cookies in your browser.

Your Rights & Data Requests

DevTree Studios does not directly store personal data through its own servers. However, third-party services integrated into this website — such as Google Analytics, Google reCAPTCHA v3, Google Drive embeds, and Formspree — may collect limited information about your visit or message.

If you wish to access, modify, or delete any data that may be associated with your usage through these services, you must contact the respective service providers (e.g., Google, Formspree) directly. If you submitted a contact form and wish your message to be removed from our email inbox, please let us know via the Contact page and we will honor your request to the best of our ability.

Data Usage

All collected data is used solely for site performance insights, spam prevention, and media functionality. We do not sell or trade user data. Data may be processed by Google and Formspree and is subject to their respective privacy policies.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with a new effective date.
